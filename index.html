About The Race

Run Into the Night

The Sundown Mile is a fun and competitive cross-country running event. It is a one mile cross country race that takes place at the Iola Winter Sports Club under the lights at sundown.

There are 4 races, youth boys, youth girls, elite men, elite women. The two youth races are made up of the kids from Vater's Youth Cross Country Camp. All other individuals would run in the elite sections.

This race was originally called the "Vater Invite" now switched to "Sundown Mile" for its 3rd annual running of the event. Vater still organizes and runs the meet.
